# City Grid — Privacy Policy

**App:** City Grid (package `in.citygrid.citizen`)  
**Operator:** City Grid (`citygridhelp@gmail.com`)  
**Last updated:** July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the **City Grid** citizen mobile app (“App”) collects, uses, and protects information when you report potholes and road hazards.

---

## 1. Who we are

City Grid is a civic reporting tool that helps citizens submit road damage reports to municipal workflows. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at **citygridhelp@gmail.com**.

---

## 2. Information we collect

### 2.1 Account information (when you sign up)

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Email address** | Create and secure your account, sign-in, password reset, email verification |
| **Password** | Authentication (stored securely by our auth provider; we do not store plain-text passwords in the app) |
| **Full name** | Collected at signup and stored with your account; **not shown publicly** on maps or recent reports |

You may browse some features as a guest, but **My Reports** and cloud sync require an account.

### 2.2 Location

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Precise location (GPS)** | Show your position on the map, tag reports with coordinates, and resolve area labels |

Location is used when you report a pothole or use map features that need your position. You can revoke location permission in Android settings; some features will not work without it.

### 2.3 Photos and report content

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Camera photos** | Close-up and wide road photos attached to your report |
| **Severity, optional note** | Describe the hazard |
| **City / area label** | Route and display the report in the correct metro |

When you **submit** a report, photos and location are uploaded to our cloud backend.

### 2.4 Anonymous reporter identity

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Reporter ID (`PW-xxx`)** | Lets you track **My Reports** across devices |

On **public maps** and **recent reports**, we show this anonymous ID — **not** your email or real name.

### 2.5 Device and technical data

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Network connectivity** | Sync reports, load maps, authenticate |
| **App version** | Shown in About; used for support and updates |

We do **not** use third-party advertising SDKs or sell your personal data.

---

## 3. How we use your information

We use collected information to:

- Accept, validate, and store pothole reports
- Show your reports on the map (with privacy-safe identity)
- Sync **My Reports** when you are signed in
- Send **report confirmation email** after a successful submission (signed-in users only)
- Show city-level weather context on the home screen
- Improve reliability and fix errors

**On-device photo checks:** Before submit, photos may be analyzed **on your phone** using rule-based checks to reduce invalid submissions. In the current app version, those checks do not send your photos to a separate commercial AI service for validation.

---

## 4. What is public vs private

| Visible publicly (map / recent reports) | Kept private |
|------------------------------------------|--------------|
| Report location (approximate pin) | Your email address |
| Photos you submitted with the report | Your password |
| Severity, status, area label | Your full name |
| Anonymous reporter ID (`PW-xxx`) | Account credentials |

---

## 5. Third-party services

We use trusted providers to run the App:

| Provider | Role | Data involved |
|----------|------|----------------|
| **Supabase** | Authentication, database, photo storage | Account, reports, photos, location |
| **Brevo** | Transactional email (report confirmation) | Email address, report summary |
| **Open-Meteo** | City weather forecast | City coordinates only (not your personal GPS) |
| **CARTO / map tile services** | Map display | Map tile requests (standard map CDN usage) |

These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy terms. We share only what is needed for the service.

---

## 6. Data retention

- **Reports and photos** are kept so municipalities can act on them and you can track status.
- **Account data** is kept while your account is active.
- We may retain limited logs for security and abuse prevention.

To request deletion of your account or personal data, email **citygridhelp@gmail.com**.

---

## 7. Your choices and rights

You can:

- **Revoke permissions** (camera, location) in Android Settings — some features will stop working
- **Delete the app** from your device (local cache may remain until cleared)
- **Request account or data deletion** by contacting us
- **Update your email** through the in-app profile flow (verification required)

If you are in India or other regions with privacy laws, you may have additional rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Contact us to exercise those rights.

---

## 8. Security

We use HTTPS for data in transit to our backend. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure; we work to protect your information using reasonable technical measures.

---

## 9. Children

The App is **not directed at children under 13**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Contact us if you believe a child has provided data and we will take appropriate steps.

---

## 10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the new version at the same URL and update the “Last updated” date. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
